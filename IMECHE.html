IMECHE Students Placement Register

For you to be able to register to the MPDS Scheme, there are 2 steps that you need to follow:
Step 1: Before you register to MPDS, you will need to register as member and the membership level is Student Affiliate. Details on how to apply can be found at www.imeche.org/affiliate

Step 2: Once you get approval on your IMechE membership, you can then enrol yourself to MPDS by completing the registration form. You only need to complete Part A, Part B and Part D, and please include your membership number and sign the declaration. Once you complete the Parts A, B and D, please email the form to Dr Perk Lin Chong (p.chong@tees.ac.uk) to proceed further.
